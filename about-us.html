What We Offering to You

Direct Way Of Justice

We believe in a legal process that is direct, efficient, and focused on results. We don’t complicate your case with unnecessary jargon or delays. Instead, we simplify complex legal issues and provide you with a clear path to justice.

Whether you're navigating a family dispute, property matter, or commercial disagreement, we ensure your rights are respected and your voice is heard. Our commitment to directness means timely updates, honest assessments, and strong representation. With us, you don’t just get a lawyer—you get a legal partner committed to resolution without detours.

Corporate Lawsuits

Navigating corporate lawsuits requires a strategic mindset and a deep understanding of both legal frameworks and business operations. We represent companies with professionalism, confidentiality, and precision. From shareholder disputes to breach of contract and compliance litigation, we advocate fiercely to protect your organization’s interests.

We work closely with executives and legal departments to craft strong litigation strategies or explore alternative dispute resolution when appropriate. Your company’s reputation and stability are our priority. With a sharp legal team and corporate acumen, we help you face lawsuits confidently—ensuring minimal disruption to your core operations.

Protecting Your Business

Your business is more than a company—it’s a vision, a livelihood, and a future. We protect what you’ve built by offering proactive legal support tailored to your industry. We draft and review contracts, ensure regulatory compliance, safeguard intellectual property, and resolve disputes before they escalate.

Whether you’re starting up or scaling up, we provide legal frameworks that empower growth and mitigate risk. With a keen understanding of Rwanda’s business landscape and global standards, we help your enterprise stay protected and future-ready—so you can focus on running your business with peace of mind.

Fight For Justice

Justice is not just a concept—it’s a right, and we’re here to help you fight for it. Whether you’ve been wronged in a personal matter, business transaction, or legal conflict, we stand firmly by your side. We approach each case with integrity, strategy, and an unshakable commitment to fairness.

Our team is equipped to handle litigation, arbitration, and mediation across various sectors. We listen, we investigate, and we advocate—relentlessly. Justice delayed is justice denied, and we’re here to make sure your case is treated with urgency and strength. Let us be your voice in the courtroom and beyond.

Relationship Based On Trust

At Fortis Associates, trust is the cornerstone of everything we do. We understand that legal matters often involve sensitive information and critical decisions. That’s why we build every client relationship on honesty, confidentiality, and reliability. From the moment you walk through our doors, we prioritize open communication and shared goals.

We explain your options clearly, set realistic expectations, and always act in your best interest. Our long-standing clients stay with us because we deliver results and stay true to our word. When you choose us, you're not just getting legal services—you’re gaining trusted advisors for every step of your journey.
