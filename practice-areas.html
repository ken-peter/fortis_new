Practice Areas

  • Home
  • Practice Areas

Corporate Governance

We support companies in the design and evaluation of effective compliance mechanisms, establishment of internal policies, incorporating respect for the law, risk management and alignment with strategic objectives.

Intellectual Property

We are actively involved in the registration, utilization and enforcement of IPR to safeguard our clients’ most valuable assets such as original ideas, designs, discoveries and inventions and creative work.

Family Law

We offer a wide range of services in relation to domestic relations, such marriage relationship, Parent-child relationship, succession, rights and duties of the parties, enforcement of orders, modification of orders, and removal of minority status.

Business Law

We have built a team of right-brained business lawyers and business consultants with business minds, who understand the business world. We assist clients to form companies, merge and take apart, create joint ventures, draft and review contracts.

Dispute Resolution

We specialize in mediation and arbitration, as well as litigation when necessary in commercial disagreement, contract breach, or partnership conflict to achieve the best possible outcome with minimal disruption to your business or personal life.

Real Estate

We cover property acquisitions and sales, leasing and tenancy agreements, zoning and land use issues, real estate development projects, and title disputes. We ensure that your property interests are protected and transactions proceed seamlessly.

Corporate Compliance

We assist businesses in adhering to local and international regulations by drafting corporate policies, ensuring compliance with tax, labor, and environmental laws, and conducting audits. Our approach helps minimize risks and maintain reputation.

Labor Law

We support both employers and employees with drafting employment contracts, handling workplace disputes, advising on compliance with labor regulations, and resolving wage and hour disputes to foster harmonious workplace environments.

Construction & Mining Law

We are committed to helping contractors and investors navigate the complexities of projects through securing and renewing licenses, advising on environmental compliance, negotiating agreements, and resolving operational disputes.
